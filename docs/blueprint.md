# **App Name**: Ahmed Fareed Photographer

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: Main Photo: Chosen by the admin in a feature in admin panel. Tagline: A clear statement describing what you do, who you serve, and your location. Featured work: A grid or gallery of your best photos to provide a quick preview of your skills.
- Portfolio Galleries: Categorize and display photography work in curated galleries (e.g., Weddings, Portraits, Landscapes) using high-quality, an optimized grid show that can show images in 3 different grid sizes (portrait(3:4) , landscape (4:3), square(1:1) ) , optimized images with a lightbox feature.
- Contact Information: Display business email address, and links to photography-relevant social media profiles for direct communication.
- About Me Section: Showcase the photographer's background, inspiration, and creative philosophy alongside a professional headshot and relevant experience.
- Awards and Exhibitions Section: Best minimalist design to showcase awards and exhibitions with pictures in slide show
- SEO Optimization: Improve discoverability with descriptive alt text for images and relevant keywords in page titles, descriptions, and content.
- Site Menu: Site menu be located left side of the page that has (home, Portfolio, about me, awards, contact me)
- Admin Panel: Only accessed by the admin and have controls and buttons and pages to upload images, choose category, downscale images to match required scales options portrait(3:4), landscape (4:3), square(1:1), and to create categories to add images to and to be shown in the main page header

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Dark gray (#333333) to provide a modern and sophisticated feel, ensuring the photographs stand out.
- Primary color: Deep blue (#2980B9) for a calm and professional feel.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#D4AC0D) for highlights and calls to action, adding a touch of sophistication and contrast against the dark background.
- Font pairing: 'Playfair' (serif) for headlines and 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text, ensuring readability and a touch of elegance.
- Minimalistic design with a focus on showcasing large, high-quality images, allowing the photographs to take center stage.
- Simple, clean, and white icons for navigation and social media links to ensure visibility against the dark background.
- Subtle fade-in animations for images to enhance the viewing experience, providing a smooth and engaging user interface.
